ABOUT ME

							
Hello, I'm Yujin Choi
 
							
a designer dedicated to creating new experiences alongside users. 
I majored in Visual Communication Design at Sookmyung Women's University and also pursued a double major in Fashion and Marketing, 
deepening my understanding of user experience, research principles, and the importance of user-centered design. 
Working on various projects, I collaborated with teams to deliver solutions tailored to user needs. 
Particularly in the project "Alkong," a group-buying service for single-person households, 
I learned the vital lesson that innovative ideas stem from analyzing user experiences. 
DThis skill significantly contributed to coordinating opinions and presenting ideas within the team.
		
I firmly believe that design must provide a positive experience to users. 
Moving forward, I will continue to pursue user-centered design, seeking new ways to improve people's lives through various services.
